Galih Adityas is an Architectural Designer with a Master's in Sustainable Building Technology from the University of Nottingham. He began his practice in 2012, building his career across Southeast Asia through hands-on involvement in design, coordination, client proposals, and project delivery.

His work focuses on the practical integration of cost-effective, energy-efficient design for low to mid-rise buildings. This includes evaluating construction methods, material strategies, and coordinated design processes that shape both performance and delivery outcomes. Galih brings experience across the full project lifecycle, from early design through completion, with consistent emphasis on documentation quality, multidisciplinary alignment, and informed technical decisions.

Beyond design and delivery, he contributes to business development through competition submissions, bid preparation, and strategic research. He also conducts post-occupancy assessments and site utilisation reviews to identify measurable improvements in operational efficiency and long-term project value.
